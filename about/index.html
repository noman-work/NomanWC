Story About Me

I wasnt always a Full Stack Developer. In a previous chapter of my life, I worked as a Front-end developer.

In 2018 Ive started work as a Full-time freelance Developer and funded a small company calledNomanWCwith 10 other Designer and Developers. I wanted to help small businesses find their voice and establish a professional but friendly online presence. I love that my work really makes a difference for entrepreneurs and family-owned businesses.

All of my work has to be meet my own satisfaction before anyone sees it (With years of experience) I call it Sophisticated Design. Its about analyzing the effectiveness of a design using objective competitive data. eCommerce is a field where this is especially effective, I love that a thoughtful redesign can directly increase a stores revenue.

I love Minimalist Design, Pizza, Tesla, Space, Workout, Taking Photo and Discipline.
