Story About Me

I wasn’t always a Full Stack Developer. In a previous chapter of my life, I worked as a Front-end developer.

In 2018 I’ve started work as a Full-time freelance Developer and funded a small company called NomanWC with 10 other Designer and Developers.  I wanted to help small businesses find their voice and establish a professional but friendly online presence. I love that my work really makes a difference for entrepreneurs and family-owned businesses.

All of my work has to be meet my own satisfaction before anyone sees it (With years of experience) – I call it Sophisticated Design. It’s about analyzing the effectiveness of a design using objective competitive data. eCommerce is a field where this is especially effective,  I love that a thoughtful redesign can directly increase a store’s revenue.

I love Minimalist Design, Pizza, Tesla, Space, Workout, Taking Photo and Discipline. 💚
